— Paperwork

Paperwork To minimize your participation in the process of cargo transportation, we take care of all the necessary documentation, customs procedures and other formalities.

- What documents will be needed?
Invoice (invoice) - contains information about the seller and the buyer of the goods, description of the goods and its price, terms of delivery, terms of payment, numbering and quantity packages
Packing List - List cargo items, indicating the location of a separate item and its weight
Certificate of conformity - certifies the quality of the goods and its compliance with the standards
Shipping Permit - Stacking Instructions and transportation of goods to a certain country. Needed for dangerous, refrigerated or valuable cargo
— Transport documents
A bill of lading is a document of maritime transport confirming the acceptance of cargo to transportation and giving the owner the right to dispose of the goods.
Waybill - a document that confirms the existence of a contract of carriage between the shipper and the carrier or his agent, as well as the transfer of risks associated with transportation from seller to buyer through a carrier.
